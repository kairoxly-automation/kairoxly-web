# Kairox FZC LLC Website Strategy

## PDF intelligence extracted
- Tagline: Your always-on digital workforce.
- Core promise: Reduce staffing costs by 70–85% while improving efficiency and business growth.
- Main pain points: response delays, missed WhatsApp/digital inquiries, inconsistent execution, hidden hiring costs, under-performing staff, long onboarding and loss of competitive momentum.
- Core solution: Kairox combines process engineering with customized AI agents to redesign workflows, automate operations and build intelligent solutions tailored to each business.
- Differentiators: built for SMEs, end-to-end delivery, custom AI agents, fast time to value, English + Arabic ready and ROI-driven pricing.
- Featured agents: Zara Sales, Maya Support, Ava Booking, Eva Accounts, Jenny Marketing, Nora BI.
- Pricing: Starter AED 1,500 setup + AED 750/month; Growth AED 2,500 setup + AED 1,500/month; Professional AED 5,000 setup + AED 3,000/month; voice agents US$0.40/min.
- Delivery journey: discovery, scope/access, build preparation, review/launch prep, launch/stabilization, growth/expansion.

## Website positioning
Kairox should feel like a premium enterprise AI partner but speak in practical SME language. The website leads with missed revenue and cost reduction, then introduces AI employees as a safer, faster alternative to expanding headcount.

## Conversion strategy
Primary CTA: Book AI Consultation. Secondary CTA: Calculate ROI. Supporting conversion assets: AI chatbot, WhatsApp CTA, pricing calculator, lead forms, industry pages and educational blog.
