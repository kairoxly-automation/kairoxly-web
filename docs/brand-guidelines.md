# Kairox Brand Identity System

## Brand personality
Innovative, reliable, enterprise-grade, future-focused, AI-first and deeply practical for UAE SMEs.

## Logo concept
A geometric K mark combines a digital monogram, leaf-like growth form and modular AI block system. It signals intelligent automation, growth and operational structure.

## Colors
- Kairox Ink: #041E2B — trust, depth and enterprise confidence.
- Deep Teal: #0B5D5F — AI, technology and reliability.
- Automation Teal: #0F766E — action, intelligence and progress.
- Strategic Gold: #C9A44A — premium value and ROI.
- Warm Cream: #F7F2E8 — approachability and UAE premium warmth.
- Stone: #ECE3D3 — calm background system.

## Typography
- Headlines: Georgia / premium serif fallback for boardroom authority.
- Body/UI: Inter / system sans for modern product clarity.

## Design language
Minimal, spacious, premium, geometric and high-trust. Use glass cards, subtle network grids, restrained gradients and clear business metrics.

## Icon style
Line icons in circular or rounded-square badges. Use Bootstrap Icons or custom one-weight icons. Avoid playful cartoon icons.

## Illustration style
Abstract AI network orbs, workflow diagrams, modular agent cards and industry-specific process visuals. Avoid generic humanoid robot stock imagery.

## Animation style
Subtle fade, lift, parallax and micro-interactions. No aggressive motion. Animation should communicate precision and premium polish.

## Usage guidelines
- Keep strong whitespace and low clutter.
- Use gold sparingly for ROI, premium tags and CTAs.
- Keep gradients controlled and dark-to-teal.
- Never overuse neon, generic AI faces or complex effects that slow load time.
