Booking Inquiry Automation for Hospitality and Events

How a hospitality team captured booking details, reduced back-and-forth and improved response consistency.

Client E — Hospitality and Events BusinessAI workflow automationHuman handover
Business challenge

The situation

  • The team handled event, booking and service inquiries across phone, website and WhatsApp.
  • Customers often sent incomplete information, requiring several manual follow-up messages.
  • Busy periods made it difficult to respond consistently while maintaining a premium customer experience.

Kairox solution

Kairox designed the automation around one clear principle: the AI employee should handle routine intake and organization, while the human team keeps control of judgment, relationship-building and sensitive decisions.

  • Created an AI booking assistant that collected event type, date, guest count, budget range, preferred package and contact details.
  • Added response templates for approved FAQs such as location, service scope, timing and next steps.
  • Built handover rules for VIP, urgent or custom-package inquiries.
  • Generated a clean inquiry summary for the booking team.

Workflow overview

Booking inquiryAI collects detailsPackage/FAQ responseTeam summaryFollow-up scheduled

Outcome areas

Reduced back-and-forthThe team received more complete booking details upfront.
Consistent premium toneCustomers received a polished initial response.
Improved inquiry routingUrgent and high-value requests were easier to identify.
Better planning dataManagement could review inquiry types and demand patterns.
“The assistant helped us keep a premium tone even when the team was busy.”

What made the workflow effective

The workflow was practical because it focused on a repeated operational bottleneck. The AI did not try to replace human judgment. It collected information, reduced delay, organized records and made handover easier.

KPIs to measure

  • Complete inquiry rate
  • Response speed
  • Qualified bookings
  • Custom requests routed
