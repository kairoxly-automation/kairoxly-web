02 The Problem

03 The Hidden Cost

Missed leads are only part of the cost.

The bigger loss comes from operational pressure: hiring expenses, uneven productivity, long learning curves, lost momentum and faster competitors.

  • Expensive hiring: higher monthly cost does not always guarantee better speed, consistency or return.
  • Uneven performance: manual work can vary depending on workload, training and availability.
  • Long learning curves: onboarding delays can slow productivity before results appear.
  • Lost momentum: missed follow-up affects revenue, customer confidence and market share.
